wordnet corpus is a lexical databse, helps in lemmatization & synonym detection.

Lexical database: repository of linguistic information, stores and organizes lexical items, such as words and their various forms, along with their meanings, grammatical properties, and other relevant linguistic features. 

Lemmatization, is a technique where we take a word and break it down to it's lemma. e.g., walk is the lemma form of walking, walks, walked
